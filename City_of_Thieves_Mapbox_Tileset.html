City of Thieves - Mapbox Tileset

The goal for this project was to create a base-map that captured the look and feel of World War II spy thriller. I used ArcGIS Pro to prepare the base layers and Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop to create custom vector symbols and patterns. I was inspired by black & white photography and the book cover of City of Thieves.

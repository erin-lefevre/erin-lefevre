My name is Erin LeFevre and I am a Cartographer and Geospatial Analyst living in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains. Here you will find a selection of my work from 2006 to 2022. I have created hundreds of different geospatial products ranging from printed wall maps, interactive mapping applications, custom GIS datasets along with custom python toolsets for processing geospatial files. I currently serve as a Senior Associate Scientist and principle Cartographer on the U.S. Extended Continental Shelf Project. In my free time I enjoy hiking, playing music, reading, and film photography. I've included from my half-frame film camera below.

My primary applications and tools include ESRI's ArcGIS Desktop, ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Adobe Illustrator, Python, JavaScript, PostgreSQL/PostGIS, Natural Scene Designer, HTML 5 and CSS. If you are interested in collaborating on a project please contact me.

Opioids, poverty, and unemployment: A formula for dying too soon - Longform editorial graphic

The goal for this project was to create a long-form editorial graphic that explored the complicated connection between opioid use, poverty and umemployment using bi-variate choropleth maps and graphics. I used ArcMap to prepare the various datasets and created the layout and graphics in ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

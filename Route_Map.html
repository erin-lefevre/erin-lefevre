International Championship Tour Bike Race, 2020 Map

The primary goal for this project was to create a basemap using satellite imagery and landcover data. The raster data-sets were processed in ArcMap and compiled in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. The secondary goal was to design a 3,000km international bike race.

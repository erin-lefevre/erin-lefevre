About:

Thank you for visiting my website. I am a cartographer and visual designer living in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains on the front-range of Colorado. I have been making maps for nearly fifteen years and am currently the lead cartographer on the U.S. Extended Continental Shelf Project. Before accepting my current position more than three years ago, I created hundreds of cartographic products for McGraw Hill (S&P Global) for nearly ten years. I have a BA in Geography from the University of Georgia with an emphasis on cartography & geospatial analysis. In addition to working full-time, I'm in the process of finishing my Master's degree in GIS & Web Map Programming at the University of Wisconsin - Madision.
If you are interested in working on a project together, please contact me at the email below. I'm open to any and all ideas related to cartography, data visualization and film photography
